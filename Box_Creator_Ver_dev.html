This Gadget Creates Two Sets of Vectors for Each Box Part on Two Separate Layers (Box and CutOut). The First (Box Layer) Vector Shows the Box Part Shape. The Second (CutOut Layer) Vector Shows & is the Actual Toolpath Set to ON the Vector.

The Datum is located in the Lower Left Hand Corner.

The Dovetail Toolpath is a Gadget Created Toolpath which cannot be Re-Calculated if Vectors have been moved.

Users Who are Familiar with the Gadget can Un-Check the Show Dovetail Re-Calc Warning for Better Productivity.
